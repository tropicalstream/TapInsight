6
Spotify (Optional)

Voice-controlled music playback. "Play jazz", "Skip this song", "What's playing?"

How to set up Spotify:
1. Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard
2. Log in with your Spotify account
3. Click "Create App"
4. App name: TapInsight, Description: AR glasses music control
5. Redirect URI: http://localhost:8888/callback
6. Check "Web API" under "Which API/SDKs are you planning to use?"
7. Click Save, then go to Settings
8. Copy the Client ID and Client Secret below
Requires a Spotify Premium account for playback control.
Both values are in your app's Settings page on the Spotify Developer Dashboard.
